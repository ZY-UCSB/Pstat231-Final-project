**Since we processed data in R, there is no csv file in processed folder.
The original data set is from Kaggle. (Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sudalairajkumar/cryptocurrencypricehistory?resource=download)

Here is a full list of variables in processed data `coins`

There are 12 variables:
(Name: Description / Data Type / Measurement Unit)
  
1.`Date(date)` : Date of observation / character / date
  
2.`ETH(eth)` : Mean price of Ethereum of each day / numeric / USD
  
3.`BTC(btc)` : Mean price of Bitcoin of each day / numeric / USD
  
4.`ADA(ada)` : Mean price of Cardano of each day / numeric / USD
  
5.`DOGE(doge)` : Mean price of Doge Coin of each day / numeric / USD
  
6.`LTC(ltc)` : Mean price of Litecoin of each day / numeric / USD
  
7.`XLM(xlm)` : Mean price of Stellar of each day / numeric / USD
  
8.`BNB(bnb)` : Mean price of Binance Coin of each day / numeric / USD
  
9.`USDT(usdt)` : Mean price of Tether of each day / numeric / USD
  
10.`XMR(xmr)` : Mean price of Monero of each day / numeric / USD
  
11.`USDC(usdc)` : Mean price of USD Coin of each day / numeric / USD
  
12.`XRP(xrp)` : Mean price of XRP of each day / numeric / USD
